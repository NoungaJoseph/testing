# Enako Outreach Organization Prospectus 2026

## Executive Summary
Enako Outreach is a community-focused humanitarian organization based in Douala, Cameroon. We design and deliver practical programs in education, clean water, health access, and family livelihood support for underserved populations.

## Problem Context
- Education barriers keep high-potential students out of school.
- Water scarcity and poor sanitation increase disease risk.
- Rural and vulnerable families face limited health access.
- Women-led and youth-led households need income pathways.

## Our Response
1. Scholarship pathways for primary, secondary, and university students.
2. Teacher recognition and school quality support.
3. Clean water infrastructure and hygiene-focused outreach.
4. Community health activities with prevention and referral support.
5. Skills and livelihood support for single mothers and youth.

## Why Partner With Enako
- Grassroots implementation in direct partnership with communities.
- Measurable model with KPI tracking and transparent reporting.
- Agile response model that balances immediate needs and long-term resilience.
- Strong alignment with SDG priorities around education, health, clean water, and gender equity.

## Program Portfolio Snapshot
- Education Support
  Scholarships, school partnerships, and teacher development.
- Water and Health
  Borehole and water access projects, health outreach support.
- Community Livelihoods
  Women and youth training, practical empowerment pathways.

## Geographic Focus
Pilot and active expansion in core communities across Cameroon, with phased scale based on readiness, partner capacity, and measured outcomes.

## Partnership Opportunities
1. Funding Partnerships
- Program sponsorship, pooled support, and milestone-based co-funding.
2. Technical Partnerships
- Skills transfer, M&E advisory, and implementation systems support.
3. Institutional Partnerships
- School, health, NGO, and local authority coordination.
4. Volunteer Partnerships
- Program support in training, mentorship, and field coordination.

## Accountability and Reporting
- Monthly operations snapshots.
- Quarterly partnership reviews.
- Annual impact and learning report.

## 2026 Partnership Priorities
- Expand scholarship coverage and improve completion pathways.
- Increase clean water and health intervention reliability.
- Strengthen school partnership onboarding and governance workflows.
